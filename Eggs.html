Nate's Recipes - My Yummy Scrambled Eggs

I made this recipe after years of perfection. I have since transformed it into what I really believe are the best scrambled eggs ever!

Ingredients

The following ingredients make one serving:

  • 4-6 eggs
  • 1 cup of cheese
  • 1/3 cup of 1% milk(2 3/4 fl ounces)
  • A pinch of Salt
  • Freshly milled Black Pepper
  • About 1/2 tbs of Paprika

Instructions

  1. Whisk eggs, milk, paprika, pepper and salt in a bowl.
  2. Heat a medium size frying pan
  3. Pour egg mixture into pan and wait until it starts setting around the edge of the pan (around 60 seconds).
  4. Using a wooden spatula, bring the mixture into the center as if it were an omelet, and let it cook for another 20 seconds.
  5. Fold contents in again, leave for 20 seconds, and repeat until the eggs are only just done.
  6. Lightly sprinkling of cheese over the eggs
  7. And serve right away with crispy bacon!

You should only make a maximum of two servings per frying pan.
